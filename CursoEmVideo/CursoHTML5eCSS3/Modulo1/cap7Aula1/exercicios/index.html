Sakura and Kaito: A Love Story

Chapter 1

The first encounter In this chapter, the protagonist, Sakura, meets her future love interest, Kaito, at school. She is charmed by his gentle smile and his fun personality, but gets nervous when he approaches her to ask for a pencil.

Chapter 2

The confession In this chapter, Sakura decides to confess to Kaito after becoming friends. She writes a letter and puts it in his backpack, hoping he will read it during the break. However, she finds out that she switched backpacks with her rival, Ayumi, who also likes Kaito.

Chapter 3

The misunderstanding In this chapter, Kaito reads Sakura’s letter in Ayumi’s backpack and thinks she is the author. He is confused, because he does not feel the same for her. He looks for Sakura to ask for advice, but she runs away from him, thinking he already knows about her feelings.

Chapter 4

The reconciliation In this chapter, Sakura and Kaito finally clear up the misunderstanding and apologize to each other. They realize that they have a lot in common and that they enjoy each other’s company. They decide to go out together after school to have some ice cream.

Chapter 5

The kiss In this chapter, Sakura and Kaito have fun on the date and get closer and closer. They walk through the park and stop in front of a fountain. Kaito holds Sakura’s hands and kisses her softly on the lips. Sakura blushes and feels happy.
